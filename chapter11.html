  • Convert these denary numbers to binary:
    • 49 = 00110001
    • 131 = 10000011
    • 127 = 01111111
    • 255 = 11111111
    • 203 = 11001011

  • Convert these denary numbers to hex:
    • 49 = 31
    • 131 = 83
    • 127 = 7F
    • 255 = FF

  • Convert these hex numbers to binary: (Split the characters up, convert them to nibbles)
    • 92 = 1001 0010
    • 5C = 0101 1100
    • FB = 1111 1011
    • ABCD = 1010 1011 1100 1101
    • FFFF = 1111 1111 1111 1111
